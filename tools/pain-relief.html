Important safety information

Dehydrated? Stop the anti-inflammatory

If you are vomiting, have diarrhoea or are not drinking enough, stop the anti-inflammatory until you are well again.

This is your personal plan

It was prescribed for you and your operation. Do not share your medicines or this plan with anyone else.

Don't double up

Take only what is listed, at the times listed. Never exceed the maximum on your prescription label. Do not take with other anti-inflammatory medicines.

Strong medicines and driving

Do not drive or operate machinery while taking a strong (opioid) top-up medicine.
