Post-arthroplasty VTE prophylaxis: aspirin vs rivaroxaban

Select the procedure and tick any factors present. The tool suggests a pathway, weighing VTE risk against bleeding risk. Set the exact thresholds and agents to your protocol.

Evidence (decision support only):
  • CRISTAL (JAMA 2022, Australian, 31 hospitals): enoxaparin 40 mg was superior to aspirin 100 mg for 90-day symptomatic VTE (1.8% vs 3.3%) after THA/TKA. Aspirin is not optimal for every patient.
  • EPCAT II (NEJM 2018): after 5 days of rivaroxaban, switching to aspirin was non-inferior to continuing rivaroxaban in selected patients - supporting aspirin for lower-risk patients after an initial potent agent.
  • Duration guide: extended prophylaxis up to ~35 days after THA and ~14 days after TKA.
Confirm against your local protocol and the hip arthroplasty review (BJA Education 2024).
