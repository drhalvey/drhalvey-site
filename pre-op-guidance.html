Recovery

Your pain relief plan

If Dr Halvey has given you a pain relief plan for your operation, you can view it online. Each plan is set in advance for a specific operation - the tool simply shows you the plan, it does not work anything out for you.

This is for patients Dr Halvey has given a plan to. The plans include strong pain medicines that are not suitable for everyone. You will need the access code provided with your plan, and you should only follow the plan your anaesthetist has confirmed is yours.
